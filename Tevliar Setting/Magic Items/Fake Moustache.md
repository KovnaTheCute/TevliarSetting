#MagicItem 
Wondrous Item, Common
Cost: 50gp
The Fake Moustache resembles an obviously fake moustache. However, when a creature places it upon their upper lip any creature that didn't see them put it on must make a DC 11 Perception check to recognize the creature. If they fail this check they only recognize the creature as familiar, and will recognize them as a unique individual unless they are told or shown otherwise.

Additionally while a creature is wearing the Fake Moustache in this way they have +1 Charisma.
