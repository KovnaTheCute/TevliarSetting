#### What is Edgedust?
Edgedust is a widespread highly addictive drug. Most of the established kingdoms outright ban the usage of Edge Dust due to its highly unpredictable magical effects however it is widespread through the underdark and is distributed illegally across Tevliar.

#### Where does it come from?
Edgedust is known as such for two reasons, the general population knows it as such due to it very often leaving it's user on the edge of death. However, the producers named it such due to it being sourced from the edge itself.

#### How is it ingested?
Edgedust is ingested in a variety of ways, it can be consumed, snorted, and injected. Each method reacts with the user in a variety of different ways depending on how it is ingested (As rolled on the tables below).
