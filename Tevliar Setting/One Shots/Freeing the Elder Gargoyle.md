Level 5 - 10 One Shot

Rough timeline
- Players traverse a series of floating islands to get to a crypt like structure
- The crypt like structure is filled with statues and Air Mephits. Each player has an amulet that causes the statues to treat them as neutral, however the mephits are hostile and they love to steal shiny magic things  from the players (the amulets). Four mephits may create an air ball that, if left alone for a turn becomes an air elemental.
- The end of the crypt contains a gargoyle wrapped in enchanted chains and inanimate. The players must input a gemstone the quest giver gave them to free the gargoyle. Alternatively remove curse and dispel magic work with a DC 15 spellcasting check and any kind of antimagic area dispels the chains but immediately starts disintegrating the gargoyle as well.
- The players must then escape, The gargoyle simply drops down and disappears
