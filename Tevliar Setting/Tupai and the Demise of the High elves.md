	There once were a proud people, tall in nature as their very essence was an embodiment of reaching beyond mortal limits to the gods above. They did not worship any but themselves. They did not know perfection for they could always reach higher. 
	
	Many of them split into their own factions, reaching towards like minded goals with their peers. However there was one goal shared by every elf regardless of individuality. 
	
	True Imortallity.

	They yearned to live forever, free of sickness, of rot and pestulance. They desired to maintain the pinicle of health, so that their pursuit of perfection could be realized.
